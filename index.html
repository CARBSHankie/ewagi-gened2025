Key Objectives

üéØ Economic & Policy Implementation

  • Energy Transition Analysis: Assess competitiveness impacts of rising electricity prices on German industry
  • Policy Design Support: Develop evidence-based policy measures balancing climate objectives with industrial competitiveness
  • Firm Behavior Modeling: Analyze heterogeneous adaptation strategies (energy efficiency, fuel switching, self-generation)
  • Macroeconomic Impact Assessment: Evaluate sectoral restructuring and overall economic development effects

üî¨ Modeling & Technical Innovations

  • Agent-Based Architecture: Enterprise-scale simulation with 20,000+ heterogeneous agents across multiple markets
  • Advanced Decision System: Policy-driven framework with sophisticated capital allocation and investment mechanisms
  • Empirical Calibration: 42-indicator validation system against real-world economic benchmarks (OECD, Eurostat, ECB)
  • HPC Production Platform: Production-ready deployment with automated data export and parameter sweep capabilities
