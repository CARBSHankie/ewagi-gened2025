Regional Data Sources

European and German regional statistical databases

  • Eurostat: NUTS-2 regional accounts and employment statistics
  • AFiD: Administrative data for research (Arbeitskreis "Forschung mit Daten der amtlichen Statistik")
  • Destatis: German Federal Statistical Office regional data

Sectoral Data Sources

Economic classification and input-output data systems

  • NACE Rev.2: European economic activity classification
  • German IO Tables: Input-output matrices and technical coefficients
  • WZ 2008: German economic sector classification system

Data Features & Structure

Geographic and sectoral data organization and coverage

  • Geographic scope: 16 German federal states (NUTS-2 level)
  • Sectoral detail: 20 sectors, 72 divisions hierarchical structure
  • Data integration: Regional-sectoral cross-tabulation matrices
