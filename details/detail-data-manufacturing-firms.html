Manufacturing Data Sources

Industrial statistics and firm-level databases

  • Destatis: German industrial statistics and firm surveys

Firm-Level Data Features

Microdata characteristics and coverage scope

  • Firm coverage: All manufacturing firms with 1+ employees
  • Size classification: Micro, small, medium, large firm categories
  • Data granularity: Plant-level production and employment data

Data Integration & Quality

Data harmonization and validation procedures

  • Harmonization: NACE Rev.2 and WZ 2008 classification alignment
  • Validation: Cross-checking with official business registers
  • Updates: Annual firm demography and structural business statistics
