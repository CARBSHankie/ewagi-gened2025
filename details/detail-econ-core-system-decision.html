System Architecture

The EWaGI Decision System enables firms to make strategic investment decisions through a policy-driven framework that mimics real-world corporate decision-making processes.

  • Decision Engine: Central coordinator that manages the entire decision-making workflow
  • Decision Policies: Business strategies that identify investment opportunities
  • Capital Allocator: Budget management that selects the best proposals within financial constraints
  • Decision Executor: Implementation engine that applies approved investments
  • Decision Context: Real-time economic data that informs all decisions

Investment Decision Types

Firms evaluate different types of strategic investments based on current market conditions and internal performance metrics.

  • Capacity Expansion: When production utilization exceeds 85%, firms evaluate expanding their production facilities
  • Energy Efficiency Upgrades: Step-wise improvements to reduce energy consumption and costs
  • Equipment Renewal: Replacement of aging machinery and technology (framework ready)
  • Process Innovation: R&D investments to improve production processes (framework ready)
  • Product Development: New product development and market expansion (framework ready)

Decision-Making Process

Firms follow a systematic 5-step process to evaluate and implement investment decisions:

  1. Market Analysis: Evaluate current economic conditions and market opportunities
  2. Investment Proposals: Generate specific investment opportunities with cost-benefit analysis
  3. Budget Allocation: Rank proposals by profitability and select within available budget
  4. Implementation: Execute approved investments and update production systems
  5. Performance Tracking: Monitor results and update financial and operational metrics

Economic Validation

The decision system ensures realistic economic behavior through rigorous financial analysis:

  • Scale Conversion: Seamlessly converts between simulation and real-world economic scales
  • NPV Analysis: 36-month cash flow projections for capacity expansion, 24-month for efficiency upgrades
  • Financial Accounting: All investments are properly recorded in firm financial statements
  • Market Pricing: Uses real-time market prices and weighted average cost of capital for discounting

System Advantages

  • Modular Architecture: Easy to add new investment strategies without disrupting existing ones
  • Extensible Framework: Clean interfaces allow for custom policy development and testing
  • High Performance: Optimized processing ensures fast decision-making even with thousands of firms
  • Rigorous Testing: Comprehensive validation through unit tests, integration tests, and scenario analysis
