Data Structure Overview

What data is stored in the EWaGI database

  • Economic agents: 3.5K firms, 41K households across 16 regions
  • Economic structure: 20 sectors, 72 divisions with production data
  • Market infrastructure: Banks, governments, malls, financial markets

Hierarchical Organization

How data is organized from macro to micro level

  • Geographic hierarchy: Country ‚Üí Region (16 German states)
  • Economic hierarchy: Sector ‚Üí Division ‚Üí Firm ‚Üí Plant
  • Agent hierarchy: Government ‚Üí Bank ‚Üí Firm ‚Üí Household

Data Usage & Applications

What the hierarchical data enables for simulation

  • Multi-scale analysis: From individual firms to national economy
  • Policy simulation: Regional and sectoral policy impact assessment
  • Economic modeling: Agent interactions across geographic and economic layers
